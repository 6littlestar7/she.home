ABOUT

Sheros is a team for women by women to help all the ladies out there.


Sheros has been working in India for 70 years, focusing on women empowerment and social exclusion. We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programmes in health, education, livelihoods and disaster preparedness and response. Sheros is collaborating with development partners in fighting COVID-19 health crisis. We are helping frontline female workers stay safe with PPE, distributing dry rations to feed the needy, raising awareness in our programme areas.
